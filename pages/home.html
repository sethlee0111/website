I'm a Ph.D. student in Mobile and Pervasive Computing Group under the supervision of Dr. Christine Julien. My research interest spans from context-aware computing to privacy preserving systems in a resource-constrained environment. Recently, I'm primarily focused on building tools and techniques that facilitates distributed machine learning in mobile platforms over decentralized data. You can find my CV here.

• Ongoing Projects

Collaborative Learning in Pervasive Computing

I'm investigating how mobile devices can collaboratively learn, personalize and maintain deep learning models via peer-to-peer communication.

Child Independent Mobility

I've participated in a project to develop a full-stack web application for motivating physical activities of children. It's deployed with an RFID tag where students can check in and record their activities according to class curriculum. It is currently being serviced at UT Elementary and soon to be serviced at Del Valle Elementary.
