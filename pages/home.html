My research interest spans from context-aware computing to privacy preserving systems in a resource-constrained environment. Recently, I am primarily focused on building tools and techniques that facilitates distributed machine learning in mobile platforms over decentralized data.

Ongoing Projects

Federated Learning

I'm working on a project that utilizes federated learning in a cellular network for a better quality of service.

Child Independent Mobility

I've participated in a project to develop a full-stack web application for motivating physical activities of children. It's deployed with an RFID tag where students can check in and record their activities according to class curriculum. It is currently being serviced at UT Elementary and soon to be serviced at Del Valle Elementary.
